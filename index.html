Chocolate Chip Cookies

Recipe From Chef Tamara

Ingredients:

1 cup butter or margerine
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 large eggs
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 cup quick oats
1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter & both sugars together with mixer
  2. Add eggs & vanilla
  3. Mix in flower, salt, baking powder & baking soda
  4. Add in the oats
  5. Mix in chocolate chips (on low)
  6. Preheat oven to 325 degrees
  7. Put cookies on tray and bake for approx. 10-12 minutes

Tips:

  • For chewier cookies, slightly underbake them and let them rest on the baking sheet for a few extra minutes after removing them from the oven.
  • You can add chopped pecans or walnuts for extra texture if needed.
  • Store cookies in an airtight container to maintain freshness.
